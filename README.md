## Data Source
Public Netflix dataset containing structured data on:
- Movies and TV shows
- Release year and content type
- Duration and runtime
- Ratings and IMDb scores
- Awards and language attributes

## Business Problem
Streaming platforms manage large content libraries, but without structured analytics it is difficult to:
- Evaluate content performance across movies and series  
- Understand how ratings vary by duration and release period  
- Identify trends in awards, content type, and viewer preferences  

Raw datasets do not provide quick, decision-ready insights.

## Dashboard Goals
- Analyze Netflix content performance using key metrics  
- Compare movies vs TV shows across ratings and duration  
- Identify trends in releases, awards, and content distribution  

## Key Features
- **KPI Cards:** Total titles, movies vs TV shows, average ratings  
- **Content Analysis:** Distribution by type, duration, and release year  
- **Ratings Analysis:** IMDb score trends and rating segmentation  
- **Awards Insights:** Award-winning content analysis  
- **Filtering:** Interactive slicers for content type and categories  

## Business Impact
- **Content Strategy:** Understand what types of content perform better  
- **Performance Analysis:** Identify rating and duration trends  
- **Decision Support:** Enable data-driven evaluation of Netflix content library
